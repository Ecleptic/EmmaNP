I am Emma

Emma Nichols-Payne is an artist from Fort Worth Texas. She grew up training in studio commercial dance attending intensives in LA, NYC and Arizona. Starting in high school she began to pursue commercial dance with the local hip-hop crew Motiv8 All-stars, directed by Michael miles, performing at local gigs and music videos.

Currently finishing up her BFA in dance at Sam Houston State university she has kept her commercial career alive while training for the contemporary modern world.

Recently she has danced back up for Nio Amnesty and performed at the 2017 Super Bowl Patriots private parties. Making her Musical theatre debut last spring in SHSUâ€™s production of Hairspray she found a new passion for the musical theater world and continues to train for Musical theatre as well as modern contemporary dance.

After graduation her initial goal is to perform in as many art forms that cross her path, big goals are Broadway and Cirque De Solei, but one must start somewhere so she is ready to set sail dancing on a cruise ship meeting all walks of life while grooving to her own beat.
